## Criteria for what makes the top 5
- **Real demand:** people are asking for it or buying adjacent products.
- **Specific niche:** not "AI prompts" generally - a defined use case and audience.
- **Defensible at $19+:** clear value beyond a free Reddit thread.
- **Buildable in <1 week:** I can ship it without inventing new infrastructure.

## Disqualifiers
- Generic "10 prompts for X" - too thin, race to bottom.
- Requires ongoing data updates (news feeds, price lists) - support burden too high for v1.
- Niche too small to support a $19 price point.
