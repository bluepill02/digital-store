Quick Win email - Loomsy example

Subject: One meeting, this week. 15 minutes. Reply with what happened.

You told me in the last email you spend 2 hours a day in standups that produce no decisions. Here's the one thing I'd try before spending a dollar on new tooling.

Run a 15-minute "decision-only" meeting with your team this week. Rules:

  • No status updates. If someone starts one, redirect them to Loomsy (or Slack, or whatever).
  • Only items that require a decision go on the agenda.
  • The output of every item is either "decided", "deferred to <person> by <date>", or "needs more info from <person>".

The principle: meetings are for decisions. Status is free to be async. Most teams have it inverted.

Do this once before Friday and reply with what happened. Even if the meeting was a disaster - especially if it was - I want to hear it. I'll feature the best replies in a future issue (with permission).
