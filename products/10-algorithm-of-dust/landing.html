Why it works

Why efficiency can't be the only value.

Efficiency is treated as a moral good in most workplaces, products, and cities. The Algorithm of Dust asks: what happens when we actually finish that thought? What gets eliminated? What's the cost to the people inside the system? It started as a short story I wrote at 2am in 2023, and turned into a novel about exactly that.
