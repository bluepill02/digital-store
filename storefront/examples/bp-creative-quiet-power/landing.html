Stillwater

"Breathe in for four.
Hold for four.
Out for six.

The pattern is the practice.
You don't have to color perfectly.
You don't have to color at all.
Sit with the page. Let your hands be still.
That's enough."

This page sits at intermediate detail - about 20-30 minutes if you color it. Less if you don't. The point is the pause.
